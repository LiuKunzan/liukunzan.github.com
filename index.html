Kunzan Liu

I am a first year Ph.D. student at MIT EECS, working at Computational Biophotonics Laboratory advised by Prof. Sixian You. I received my B.E. degree in EE from Tsinghua University in 2022, where I worked on dimensionality reduction for wireless communications with Prof. Linglong Dai.

My current research interests lie in the fields of multiphoton microscopy and computational imaging for biological understandings.
